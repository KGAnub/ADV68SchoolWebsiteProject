Welcome to Chordia Jain School

Chordia Jain School is a co-educational day school which is affiliated to the Matriculation Board. The school has a vast campus that spreads over an area of 8 acres and has facilities such as a playground, laboratories, activity areas, and an auditorium to boost co-curricular activities. The school aims to develop the individuality of the students spiritually, emotionally, academically, socially and physically so that they will become well-balanced persons who can face challenges in all walks of life. The school has structured its curriculum in such a way that it gives a multidimensional approach to make the learning process effective and enjoyable. It serves students from Pre Primary to Class X. The campus facilities include smart classrooms, spacious auditorium, Science Lab, Computer Lab, library, provides transport facility. Through Inter-House Competitions, the school also encourages various ranges of sports activities and special courts separately to encourage all kinds of sports. The school provides a perfect ambience for the students to ‘think good and act well’. ‘Catch them doing good’ is the primary spirit of the faculty; the students are openly and loudly praised for their achievements and privately counselled on their lapses from time to time. The school provides a holistic and progressive education in tune with the latest developments in education and co-curricular activities. The school opens for admissions every year in May and conducts an entrance exam for the same.
